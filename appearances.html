Appearances in movies 

King Ghidorah has appeared in 13 live-action and animated films, solidifying his role as Godzilla's most iconic rival. His cinematic history spans 10 Japanese Toho productions, one American Monsterverse blockbuster, and two animated features. Across these eras, he has transitioned from an alien invader in the 1960s to a heroic guardian in 2001 and an interdimensional entity in 2018. While he is almost always the primary antagonist, his appearances also include cameos and variants like Mecha-King Ghidorah, making him a cornerstone of the kaiju genre.
