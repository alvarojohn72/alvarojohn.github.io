Powers & Abilities 

Gravity Beams (or Bolts): Ghidorah fires concentrated, crackling beams of yellow electrical/gravitational energy from all three heads. These beams are devastating upon impact and possess immense concussive force.

 Weather and Storm Manipulation: Due to his extreme bioelectrical nature, his presence causes storms. He distorts normal weather patterns to generate Category 6 superstorms, hurricanes, and massive tornadoes simply by flying or awakening. 

Energy Absorption and Redirection: Ghidorah can absorb massive amounts of electrical power, including lightning strikes or energy from atomic pulses, channeling it to supercharge himself or redirecting it back at enemies. 

Three Independent Heads: Having three heads allows him to attack, defend, and grab targets simultaneously. Because they act as separate minds, it is incredibly difficult to blindside him, and he can use one head to bite while another neutralizes limbs. 

Constriction: Like a multi-headed cobra, Ghidorah can wrap his long, flexible necks around an opponent to choke them or pin them down. 

Flight and Dropping: He possesses immense wingspans that allow for sustained flight. He frequently uses his immense physical strength to lift rival Titans (such as Godzilla) high into the atmosphere and drop them to their demise
